About CarboBon

We use dMRV technology, machine learning and human expertise for meaningful decarbonization: from driving global carbon compliance and accreditation for industries and institutions, for the geospatial monitoring of forests and wetlands.

Team

Meet our founders

Navid Haider

Chief Executive Officer

Navid, Co-founder and CEO of CarboBon, leads the mission to build Bangladesh’s carbon market and bring data-driven climate action to the Global South. After supporting major brands in their decarbonization journeys at Voltus, he now guides CarboBon’s use of AI and ML to help businesses cut emissions and create measurable impact.

Prottay Hasan

Chief Strategy Officer

Prottay Hasan, Chief Strategy Officer at CarboBon, makes complex ideas approachable and actionable. With experience turning academic research into businesses, he brings fresh perspectives and strategic clarity. At CarboBon, he’s focused on scaling solutions that drive meaningful change across sustainability and innovation.

Gunjan Barua

Chief Technology Officer

As Co-founder and CTO, Gunjan Barua leads the tech that powers CarboBon’s mission. He built CarboBon’s DMRV system and leads work that turns data into tools for fighting deforestation and tracking carbon. Thoughtful and purpose-driven, he helps keep our technology rooted in real-world impact.

Asef M. Redwan

Senior Science Lead

Asef brings a passion for solving environmental challenges through science and innovation. Having led cutting-edge research on bioremediation and rare earth metals, he now guides CarboBon’s science team to turn complex ideas into real-world solutions that help build a mor
